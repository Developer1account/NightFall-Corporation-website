Description

NightFall Corporation is an SCP RP faction which hosts daily events and trainings, we plan to expand into multiple games and eventually create our own map to host custom events and more.

Made by Peterhans

Role Structure

# THE ADMINISTRATOR
Responsible for the coordination of all units during deployments and the oversight of major administrative and structural decisions within the Corporation.

# THE COUNCIL
The highest-ranking members of the server, operating directly under The Administrator.

# THE ARCHITECT
Lead designer of the revamped facility and its numerous morphs.

# Executive Director
Supervises Division Overseers.

# Division Overseer
Supervises Unit Overseers.

# Unit Overseer
Leads a combat unit.

# Veteran Operative
Highly skilled faction member.

# Senior Operative
Experienced unit member.

# Operative
Main combat force.

# Probationary Operative
New members under evaluation.

# NOTABLE MENTIONS
GOONING gif creator: our server's artist
War reporter: Responsible for recording our NON war crimes
