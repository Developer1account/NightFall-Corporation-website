Description

NightFall Corporation is an SCP RP faction which hosts daily events and trainings, we plan to expand into multiple games and eventually create our own map to host custom events and more.

Made by Peterhans

Role Structure

# THE ADMINISTRATOR
Responsible for the coordination of all units during deployments and the oversight of major administrative and structural decisions within the Corporation.

# THE COUNCIL
The highest-ranking members of the server, operating directly under The Administrator. Responsible for the overall management of the server and the resolution of member-related matters. (16 years+)

# THE ARCHITECT
Lead designer of the revamped facility and its numerous morphs. (Usually a twink)

# Executive Director
A high-ranking member entrusted with supervising Division Overseers and enforcing the Nightfall Code of Conduct upon those who fail to comply.

# Division Overseer
Responsible for the supervision of four Unit Overseers and their respective operatives. Division Overseers form the backbone of the administrative branch and represent the lowest rank within the Executive Section.

# Unit Overseer
Senior Operatives who have demonstrated exceptional commitment to the cause and are granted the authority to lead a unit of five or more personnel in combat operations.

# Veteran Operative
Highly skilled and proven members of the faction, granted the privilege of serving within the most elite units of the Corporation.

# Senior Operative
An experienced and reliable member of the unit, regarded as a valuable asset during combat and coordinated operations.

# Operative
Operatives form the backbone of our forces and are responsible for carrying out the directives issued by The Council.

# Probationary Operative
Newly inducted members of the Corporation who are undergoing evaluation and must prove their discipline, loyalty, and capability in the field.
